Terms of Service

Important information about our rental policies.

Standard rental periods are 24 hours starting from the time of setup. Overtime rates apply at ₱25 per additional hour (unless pre-arranged).

  • Delivery window: 8:00 AM – 12:00 PM
  • Pick-up window: Next day, 9:00 AM – 1:00 PM
  • The renter must be present at the time of delivery to sign the inspection report.

The renter assumes full responsibility for the equipment upon delivery. RentIT is not liable for damage due to negligence.

  • Keep equipment dry and protected from weather elements.
  • Only authorized personnel may move the main console once installed.
  • Do not allow children to operate the equipment unsupervised.

We understand plans change. Our refund structure is as follows:

7+ Days Notice

Full refund of deposit.

3-6 Days Notice

50% refund of deposit.

Less than 48 Hours

No refund.

  • Wireless Microphone: ₱800 per unit (lost / irreparable)
  • Speaker: ₱3,000
  • LCD Monitor / Display: ₱4,500
  • Main Console: ₱8,000 - ₱15,000 depending on model
